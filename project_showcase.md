# Procurement Intelligence Copilot

## Problem

Procurement organizations often rely on spreadsheets and manual supplier reviews to identify risk.

This creates delays in identifying:

- High-risk suppliers
- Spend concentration
- Supply continuity threats
- Alternative sourcing opportunities

---

## Objective

Build a procurement analytics platform capable of:

- Scoring supplier risk
- Benchmarking suppliers
- Identifying portfolio exposure
- Recommending mitigation actions
- Supporting procurement decision-making

---

## Solution

Developed a modular procurement intelligence platform using:

- Python
- Pandas
- Streamlit
- Plotly

The platform analyzes supplier performance and generates actionable procurement insights.

---

## Key Capabilities

### Supplier Risk Scoring

Risk calculated using:

- Delivery performance
- Quality metrics
- Lead times
- Supplier responsiveness

### Portfolio Risk Exposure

Combines:

- Supplier risk
- Annual spend

to identify the suppliers that represent the largest business exposure.

### Supplier Benchmarking

Compares suppliers against category peers.

### Executive Procurement Brief

Automatically generates executive summaries and management recommendations.

### Procurement Analyst

Natural language procurement assistant capable of answering portfolio questions.

### Supplier Comparison

Side-by-side supplier evaluation for sourcing decisions.

### Scenario Analysis

Simulation engine for evaluating supplier improvement opportunities.
