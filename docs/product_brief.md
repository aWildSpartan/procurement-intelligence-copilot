# Procurement Intelligence Copilot

## Problem

Procurement teams often rely on spreadsheets and fragmented information to evaluate suppliers, making it difficult to identify supplier risk and justify sourcing decisions.

## Solution

An AI-powered decision-support platform that analyzes supplier performance data, ranks supplier risk, and provides procurement recommendations through dashboards and natural language queries.

## Target Users

- Procurement Analysts
- Strategic Buyers
- Category Managers
- Supply Chain Analysts

## MVP Features

- Supplier performance dashboard
- Supplier risk scoring
- Supplier ranking
- AI-generated supplier insights

## Success Metrics

- Supplier risk identified in seconds instead of manual review
- Explainable supplier recommendations
- Interactive analytics dashboard
